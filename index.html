What is Lengo?

Our Mission

Empowering youth for sustainable development in Africa.

Our Vision

To promote youth involvement in community and global development.

Lengo is a Swahili word that means ‘Goal’. Lengo Africa is an organisation committed to bringing young creatives together towards the attainment of SDGs in Africa. We are committed to providing a platform for young people to network and work together in coming up with sustainable solutions to challenges being faced in their communities. We seek to solve these challenges by gearing the youth to work towards community development in their diverse spheres of passion.

Lengo Africa aims at building a strong support system for young changemakers to connect and grow together. As such, we are focused on developing leadership, creativity, empathy, entrepreneurship and participation among the youth. We strongly believe that all global issues can be solved by empowering the youth.

Lengo Africa is a platform for young changemakers to network and work together in developing their interests as well as their communities. A platform that will facilitate sharing, development and implementation of ideas by young people to work as sustainable and workable solutions to existing and emerging challenges in Africa.
